# Code of Conduct

Be direct, respectful, and useful.

## Expected

- Focus criticism on code, docs, and technical decisions.
- Keep issues and pull requests actionable.
- Assume good intent unless there is clear evidence otherwise.
- Disagree with specifics and propose a path forward.

## Not Accepted

- Harassment, threats, slurs, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information or credentials.
- Repeated off-topic comments.
- Hostile comments that block maintainers or contributors from doing the work.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior disrupts the project.
